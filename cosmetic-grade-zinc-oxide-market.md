There are two main types of cosmetic grade zinc oxide: indirect process and nano zinc oxide. </p><p>Indirect process zinc oxide is produced by the chemical reaction of zinc metal with oxygen, resulting in a fine white powder. It is widely used in cosmetics for its ability to provide broad-spectrum UV protection and to absorb excess oil on the skin.</p><p>Nano zinc oxide refers to zinc oxide particles that have been reduced to nanoscale size. This gives the ingredient enhanced transparency and increased sun protection capabilities compared to regular zinc oxide. Nano zinc oxide is commonly used in sunscreen formulations, providing effective and safe protection against harmful UV rays.</p></p>
