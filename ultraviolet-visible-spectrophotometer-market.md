<p><p>An ultraviolet-visible (UV-Vis) spectrophotometer is a scientific instrument used to measure the intensity of light absorbed by a sample in the ultraviolet and visible regions of the electromagnetic spectrum. UV-Vis spectrophotometry is widely used in various industries such as life sciences, pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, environmental testing, and food and beverages.</p><p>The future outlook of the UV-Vis spectrophotometer market is promising.
