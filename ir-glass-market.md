<p><p>The IR glass market is segmented into three categories based on wavelength range: 1~3μm, 3~5μm, and 8~14μm. The 1~3μm segment handles infrared light with shorter wavelengths, making it suitable for applications like laser technology and spectroscopy. The 3~5μm segment deals with mid-wave infrared light and is commonly used in thermal imaging and gas sensing applications. The 8~14μm segment, also known as long-wave infrared, is commonly utilized in thermal imaging cameras and night vision devices due to its ability to accurately detect temperature differences and low light conditions.</p></p>
