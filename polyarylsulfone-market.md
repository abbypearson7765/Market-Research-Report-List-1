<p><p>The Polyarylsulfone market includes various types such as PPSU, PSU, PEI, and PESU. These are high-performance thermoplastics widely used in industries. PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone) offers excellent heat resistance, purity, and impact strength, making it suitable for medical and aircraft applications. PSU (Polysulfone) provides good dimensional stability and transparency, used in electrical and automotive components. PEI (Polyetherimide) has high strength, chemical resistance, and electrical properties, commonly utilized in electronics and aerospace industries. PESU (Polyethersulfone) offers excellent heat resistance and flame retardancy, widely used in electrical connectors and filtration applications.</p></p>
