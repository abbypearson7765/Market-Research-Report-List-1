<p><p>Image based barcode scanner, also known as 2D barcode scanner or imaging barcode scanner, is a type of barcode scanner that uses image capture technology to read barcodes. Unlike traditional laser barcode scanners, image-based scanners capture an image of the barcode and then analyze it to decode the information.</p><p>The image-based barcode scanner market is currently witnessing significant growth and is expected to continue this trend in the future. The increasing adoption of image-based barcode scanners across various industries, such as retail, healthcare, transportation, and logistics, is the major factor driving market growth. These scanners offer advantages over traditional laser scanners, such as the ability to read damaged or poorly printed barcodes, as well as the capability to read 2D barcodes, which are becoming more popular in applications like mobile payments and digital tickets.</p><p>Additionally, the rising demand for automation and improved efficiency in these industries is also fueling the market growth. Image-based barcode scanners can be easily integrated with other systems, such as inventory management and point of sale (POS) systems, to enhance productivity and reduce errors.</p><p>The growing e-commerce industry and the need for efficient sorting and tracking of products are further contributing to the market growth. Image-based barcode scanners offer real-time tracking and enhanced visibility of products throughout the supply chain.</p><p>However, the market may face challenges due to the high cost of these scanners compared to traditional laser barcode scanners. Moreover, the presence of alternative technologies like RFID and NFC may hinder the growth of the image-based barcode scanner market to some extent.</p><p>Overall, the future outlook of the image-based barcode scanner market looks promising. The increasing adoption of automation and digitization across various industries, coupled with the advantages offered by image-based barcode scanners, is expected to drive the market growth. The market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 13.3% during the forecasted period.</p></p>
