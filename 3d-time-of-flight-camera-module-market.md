<p><p>A 3D Time of flight (ToF) Camera Module is a device that uses ToF technology to measure the distance between the camera and objects in its field of view. This technology is widely used in applications such as augmented reality, gaming, autonomous vehicles, robotics, and industrial automation.</p><p>The future of the 3D ToF Camera Module Market appears promising, primarily driven by the increasing demand for advanced imaging and depth sensing technologies in various industries.
