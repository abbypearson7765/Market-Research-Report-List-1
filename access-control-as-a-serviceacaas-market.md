<p><p>Access Control as a Service (ACaaS) is a cloud-based security solution that provides organizations with centralized control and management of physical access to facilities. With more companies adopting remote working policies and implementing flexible access control measures, the demand for ACaaS is likely to increase in the coming years.</p><p>The market is also seeing advancements in technologies such as biometrics and artificial intelligence, which are further fuelling the growth of ACaaS.
