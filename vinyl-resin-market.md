<p><p>Vinyl resin is a type of synthetic resin derived from vinyl chloride monomer (VCM), which undergoes polymerization to form a durable, cost-effective, and versatile material. Additionally, the increasing demand for lightweight and fuel-efficient vehicles is expected to boost the market's growth, as vinyl resins are used in automotive interiors, exterior trims, and underbody coatings.</p><p>Moreover, the packaging industry is witnessing significant growth, driven by the e-commerce sector and demand for sustainable packaging materials. Vinyl resin offers excellent barrier properties and is recyclable, making it suitable for various packaging applications such as bottles, containers, and films.
