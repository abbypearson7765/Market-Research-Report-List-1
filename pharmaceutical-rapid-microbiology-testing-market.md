<p><p>The pharmaceutical rapid microbiology testing market includes various types of testing methods. Immunology-based methods use antibodies to detect and identify microorganisms. Nucleic acid-based amplification tests amplify and detect the genetic material of microorganisms. Mass spectrometry-based methods analyze the molecular composition of microorganisms. Flow cytometry-based methods count and sort microorganisms based on their physical and chemical properties. Growth-based detection technologies rely on the growth of microorganisms in culture media. Biochemical tests measure the metabolic activities of microorganisms. These different methods help pharmaceutical companies rapidly and accurately assess the microbial quality of their products.</p></p>
