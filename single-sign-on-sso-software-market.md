<p><p>Single Sign On (SSO) software is a security technology that allows users to access multiple applications and systems with a single set of login credentials. It eliminates the need for separate usernames and passwords for each system, thereby simplifying the authentication process for both users and IT administrators.</p><p>The future of the SSO software market looks promising due to several factors. Firstly, the increasing adoption of cloud-based applications and services is driving the demand for SSO solutions. As organizations move their applications and data to the cloud, they need a secure and efficient way to manage user authentication and access control.</p><p>Moreover, the growing emphasis on cybersecurity and data protection is propelling the demand for SSO software. With the increasing frequency and sophistication of cyber threats, organizations are investing in robust identity and access management solutions to ensure the security of their systems and networks.</p><p>Additionally, the rise of remote work and bring-your-own-device (BYOD) policies is fuelling the need for SSO software. As employees access corporate resources from various devices and locations, SSO ensures seamless and secure access while maintaining user convenience.</p><p>According to the provided information, the SSO software market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 13.7% during the forecasted period. This growth can be attributed to the factors mentioned earlier, along with the increasing adoption of mobile devices and the evolving regulatory landscape.</p><p>Overall, the future outlook of the SSO software market remains positive, driven by the need for enhanced security, streamlined user experience, and efficient access management in an increasingly digital and interconnected world.</p></p>

It provides flexibility, scalability, and easy access from any location or device with an internet connection. On the other hand, web-based SSO software relies on a local server infrastructure, where the software and user authentication process are hosted on-site. It offers more control and customization options but may require more maintenance and resources.</p></p>
