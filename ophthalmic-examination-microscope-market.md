<p><p>The Ophthalmic Examination Microscope market encompasses two main types: On Casters and Fixed. On Casters microscopes are designed with wheels attached, allowing easy mobility and flexibility within the clinic or hospital setting. They can be effortlessly moved from one room to another, ensuring convenience and efficient use of space. On the other hand, Fixed microscopes are stationary and permanently installed in a specific location. This type is more suitable for practices with a dedicated examination room and does not require frequent relocation.</p></p>
