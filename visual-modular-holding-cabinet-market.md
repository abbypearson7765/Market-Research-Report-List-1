<p><p>The Visual Modular Holding Cabinet market includes various types such as Bottom Heat and Top and Bottom Heat. Top and Bottom Heat holding cabinets, on the other hand, offer heat from both the top and bottom, enhancing food quality and keeping it warm for longer duration. These different market types cater to the specific needs of food service businesses, allowing them to maintain the desired temperature and quality of their dishes.</p></p>
