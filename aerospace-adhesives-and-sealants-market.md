<p><p>Aerospace adhesives and sealants can be categorized into two types: reactive adhesives and non-reactive adhesives. Reactive adhesives undergo a chemical reaction to convert them into a solid, providing excellent bonding strength. These adhesives are commonly used in critical aerospace applications. On the other hand, non-reactive adhesives do not undergo a chemical reaction and rely on physical bonding mechanisms. They provide good flexibility and resistance to environmental factors. Both types play a crucial role in aerospace manufacturing, ensuring strong and durable bonds for various components and structures.</p></p>
