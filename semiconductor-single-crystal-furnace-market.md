<p><p>The Semiconductor Single Crystal Furnace market comprises two major types: CZ Single Crystal Furnace and FZ Single Crystal Furnace. CZ Single Crystal Furnace refers to a method of growing semiconductor single crystals by pulling them from a molten material using a seed crystal. This technique is widely used due to its ability to produce large and high-quality crystals. On the other hand, FZ Single Crystal Furnace utilizes the floating zone technique, where a small molten zone is passed through the material, resulting in the growth of single crystals with extremely low impurities. Both types are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
