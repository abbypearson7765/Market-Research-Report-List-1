<p><p>A non-woven folding machine is a technological tool designed to fold non-woven fabrics efficiently. The increasing demand for non-woven fabrics in various sectors is a significant driving factor for the market's expansion. The healthcare industry, in particular, is witnessing a surge in the usage of non-woven fabrics for medical supplies, such as masks, gowns, and drapes, which is likely to propel the growth of the folding machine market.</p><p>Moreover, the growing awareness about environmental sustainability is leading to the rising demand for non-woven fabrics as an alternative to traditional woven or knitted textiles. Non-woven fabrics are considered eco-friendly, as they can be easily recycled and do not require the same energy-intensive manufacturing processes as traditional fabrics. This shift towards sustainable materials is expected to boost the demand for non-woven folding machines.</p><p>Additionally, technological advancements and automation in the textile industry are facilitating the development of more efficient and faster non-woven folding machines.
