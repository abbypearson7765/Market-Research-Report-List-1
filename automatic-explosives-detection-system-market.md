<p><p>The Automatic Explosives Detection System (EDS) market can be divided into several types. Vapour Detection involves the detection of explosive materials by analyzing their vapour emissions. Nuclear Quadrupole Resonance (NQR) technology uses nuclear magnetic resonance to identify explosives based on their unique molecular structure. Muon Tomography Technology utilizes cosmic ray muons to create images and detect hidden explosives. X-ray Techniques employ X-ray imaging technology to scan and identify explosive substances. Lastly, there are various other detection technologies that include trace detection, thermal neutron analysis, and electromagnetic induction among others.</p></p>
